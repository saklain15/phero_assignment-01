Our Donation plan

We will give our best to distribute your donation money by following these three subjects.

Food for a family

Food and medicine are very important for flooded people. We provide them with all kinds of life-supporting goods, which will directly help them to overcome the current situation.

Shelter for a family

We will ensure safe area. Most of the houses take over by water. Shelter means protection during period of crisis. The people of Bangladesh, a country prone to natural disasters, are familiar with the ‘Cyclone Shelter’. But the concept of shelter will solve gradually.

Education for a child

Children need to be properly taught about the dangers of flooding, what to do, what not to do, and how to best keep themselves safe and alive. It should be explained to them in language that they understand, in a manner designed to educate and not simply frighten. In addition, adults should be proper role models when it comes to heeding the advice and warnings as issued by the National Weather Service and taking all appropriate safety measures.
